Player Profile: Virat Kohli

Full Name: Virat Kohli

Born: November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India

Role: Batsman

Career Statistics:

  • Matches: 102 Tests, 254 ODIs, 115 T20Is
  • Runs: 8,300+ (Test), 12,000+ (ODI), 3,000+ (T20I)
  • Centuries: 27 (Test), 43 (ODI), 1 (T20I)

Notable Achievements:

  • ICC ODI Player of the Year (2012, 2017)
  • ICC Test Player of the Year (2018)
  • Fastest player to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in ODIs

Top Matches

Here are some memorable matches featuring Virat Kohli:

  • India vs Australia, 2nd Test, 2014 - Kohli scored a brilliant 115 runs in his first Test as captain.
  • India vs Pakistan, ICC Champions Trophy, 2017 - Kohli's exceptional performance helped India clinch the title.
